## Description

A loose network of druids and rangers who protect the wild places of [[The Western Reach]]. They operate quietly — most people have heard rumors of forest guardians but don't know the Circle by name.

<!-- GOALS & METHODS: What do they want? How do they

## Goals & Methods

The Circle believes the balance between civilization and wilderness must be actively maintained. They monitor logging operations, guard sacred groves, and occasionally sabotage settlements that expand too aggressively into the forest.

They communicate through animal messengers and maintain hidden meeting places in ancient groves.
