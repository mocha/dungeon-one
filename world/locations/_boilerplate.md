## Description

A small mining town nestled in a valley between wooded hills. Timber-framed buildings line a single main street that runs from the town gate to the mine entrance at the valley's head. Smoke from the smelters hangs in the air on still days.

## Details

Thornhaven exists because of the iron mine. Most residents are miners, loggers, or the merchants who supply them. The town is governed by [[Baron Aldric Cane]], a distant landlord who visits twice a year to collect taxes.

The only notable establishments are [[The Pickaxe Inn]], [[Maren Ashveil]]'s apothecary, and the mine offices.
