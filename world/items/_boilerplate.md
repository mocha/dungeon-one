# Moonpetal Salve

<!-- DESCRIPTION: What does this item look like?

## Description

A pale blue ointment stored in small clay pots sealed with beeswax. It has a faint floral scent and feels cool to the touch. Applied to wounds, it accelerates natural healing overnight.

<!-- PROPERTIES: What does it do mechanically?
     As specific or vague as you want. -->
## Properties

When applied before a long rest, the user regains additional hit points equal to their Constitution modifier. One pot contains enough salve for three applications.

## Notes

- [[Maren Ashveil]] makes these from moonpetal flowers that only bloom under a full moon
- She keeps a limited supply and won't sell for gold — she trades for rare herbs
- The salve loses potency after one month if the seal is broken
