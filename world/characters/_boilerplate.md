## Description

A wiry half-elf with soil-stained hands and sharp green eyes. She wears a leather apron over simple traveling clothes and always smells faintly of rosemary and something metallic.

## Background

Maren runs the only apothecary in [[Thornhaven]], supplying remedies to miners and loggers who work the surrounding hills. She's a ranking member of [[The Verdant Circle]] but keeps that affiliation quiet — the local baron distrusts druids.

She came to Thornhaven five years ago from [[The Ashveil Coast]], though she doesn't talk about why she left.
