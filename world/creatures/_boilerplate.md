## Description

A massive wolf-like creature with a ridge of dark, thorn-like spines running from its skull to the base of its tail. Thornback wargs are smarter than natural wolves — their yellow eyes show a cunning, predatory intelligence.

<!-- BEHAVIOR: How does it act? How does it hunt?

## Behavior

Thornback wargs hunt in packs of 3-6, using coordinated tactics to drive prey toward ambush positions. They're territorial and mark their range by gouging tree bark with their spines. They've been known to avoid armed groups unless desperate or provoked.

## Notes

- Packs have been moving closer to [[Thornhaven]] in recent months — something is driving them from the deep forest
- Their spines can be harvested for poison (a skilled herbalist like [[Maren Ashveil]] would know how)
- Legends say the original wargs were wolves corrupted by wild magic
