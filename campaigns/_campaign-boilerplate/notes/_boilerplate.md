## Scenes to Prep

- **The forest path** — Description of the woods north of [[Thornhaven]]. Dense, old-growth, increasingly tangled as they go deeper. Signs of [[Thornback Warg]] territory (gouged bark, tracks).
- **The warg encounter** — A pack of 4 wargs blocks the path. Can be fought, avoided with stealth, or possibly calmed by someone who understands animals.
- **The grove fragment** — A clearing where the trees are ancient and twisted. The air feels wrong. A pool of dark water in the center that shouldn't be there.

## Questions to Answer

- Does Maren come with the party or stay behind?
- What happens if the party tries to purify the grove?
- Is there a physical entrance to the underground here, or is the corruption seeping through the earth?

## Ideas

- The grove could have a guardian — a corrupted treant that attacks but could be freed
- If the party brings back a sample of the dark water, Maren could identify it as fiendish
- A dying animal near the grove could be a sympathetic moment that shows the stakes
