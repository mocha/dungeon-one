## Recap

The party arrived in [[Thornhaven]] after three days on [[The North Road]]. They found rooms at [[The Pickaxe Inn]] and heard rumors about strange sounds from the mines. [[Maren Ashveil]] approached them at the inn, asking if they'd be willing to investigate something in the forest north of town.

[[Baron Aldric Cane]]'s tax collector was also in town, and tensions were high between miners and the baron's men.
