# Find the Lost Grove

<!-- SUMMARY: What's the quest? What does the quest giver

## Summary

[[Maren Ashveil]] has asked the party to help her locate a fragment of [[The Elder Grove]] that she believes is hidden in the hills north of [[Thornhaven]]. She says the grove is dying and needs to be tended before its magic fades entirely.

<!-- DETAILS: What the party knows, what they don't know,
     complications, possible approaches. -->
## Details

Maren provided a rough description of the grove's location but no map. She warned that the forest has become dangerous — [[Thornback Warg]] packs are more aggressive than usual, and she suspects something is corrupting the area.

What the party doesn't know: the grove fragment sits directly above the unsealed fiendish incursion from [[The Sundering of the Elder Grove]]. The corruption they'll encounter is coming from below, not from the surface.
